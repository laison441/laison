Most Played Games

Grand Theft Auto V

Grand Theft Auto V is an action-adventure game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. It is one of the best-selling games of all time.

Fortnite

Fortnite is a battle royale game developed by Epic Games. It has gained massive popularity for its engaging gameplay and frequent updates.

League of Legends

League of Legends is a multiplayer online battle arena game developed and published by Riot Games. It has a large competitive scene and a massive player base.

Popular Games

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild is an action-adventure game developed and published by Nintendo. It is acclaimed for its open-world gameplay and intricate design.

Animal Crossing: New Horizons

Animal Crossing: New Horizons is a life simulation game developed and published by Nintendo. It offers a relaxing gameplay experience where players can build and customize their own island.

Cyberpunk 2077

Cyberpunk 2077 is an open-world, action-adventure RPG developed and published by CD Projekt. Despite its rocky launch, it remains a popular title for its immersive world and deep storytelling.

Latest Games

Resident Evil Village

Resident Evil Village is a survival horror game developed and published by Capcom. It continues the story of Ethan Winters as he faces new horrors in a mysterious village.

Halo Infinite

Halo Infinite is a first-person shooter game developed by 343 Industries and published by Xbox Game Studios. It is the latest entry in the acclaimed Halo series and features an open-world campaign.

Deathloop is an action-adventure game developed by Arkane Studios and published by Bethesda Softworks. The game features a time loop mechanic and a unique art style.

First Ever Game

Pong

Pong, released in 1972 by Atari, is one of the earliest arcade video games. It simulates table tennis and was one of the first video games to gain widespread popularity.

Best Game Companies

Nintendo

Nintendo is a Japanese video game company known for creating some of the most iconic game franchises, including Mario, The Legend of Zelda, and Pokémon.

Sony Interactive Entertainment

Sony Interactive Entertainment is a subsidiary of Sony Corporation, responsible for the PlayStation brand. It has developed many successful game series, including Uncharted, The Last of Us, and God of War.

Rockstar Games is an American video game publisher known for creating critically acclaimed games such as Grand Theft Auto, Red Dead Redemption, and Bully.
